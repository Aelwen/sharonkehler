This is a first time author experience for Sharon Kehler. Her lifetime of journaling has inspired her to publish this memoir. Her role as wife, mother and grandmother is her deepest passion. Her sojourn into her past brings the reader deeper into her voyage as the events progress.

The Day You Went Away is a raw, personal journey written from the perspective of a grieving mother. The news of her son's death along with his fiancé in a cabin in the Blue mountains of Australia send her spiraling into a tunnel of darkness that only her faith in God can bring her back into the light. Her story takes us back into her memory walk of where it all began. The reminiscent approach sends the reader into a nostalgic state themselves. Her raw truthful experience tells a captivating story that can inspire others in their own odyssey. (A spiritual wandering.)

She concludes with the current state of her life as well as her family. Her quest for closure to this tragic experience is to share with readers is a hopeful desire to find comfort in the memories of the life her child lived and not focus on the void that his death has caused.

About the Author

Sharon Kehler works as a massage and nail technician in her hometown of Grunthal, Manitoba. She is passionate about her family, in particular her two grandchildren who are the sunshine in her life.

Her passions extend to her crafts: sewing, quilting, and reading. She spends plenty of time outdoors which she considers quite therapeutic. Sharon loves to write in her journal, which has turned into her first book.
